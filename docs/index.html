Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who juggle multiple WhatsApp accounts — personal, work, business — without the tab chaos.

Multiple Accounts

Each account runs in its own isolated session. Add as many as you need, each with its own QR login.

Toggle Sidebar

Hide the WhatsApp chat list instantly with ⌘B. Focus on the conversation that matters.

Flexible Tabs

Switch between horizontal top tabs and vertical left tabs. Pick whichever layout suits your workflow.

Account Management

Right-click any tab to rename, reload, or remove it. Name your accounts when you add them.

Persistent Sessions

Your login survives app restarts. Scan QR once, stay connected. Sessions are isolated per account.

Native macOS

Hidden title bar, traffic light controls, native context menus. Feels like it belongs on your Mac.

Getting Started

Up and running
in 30 seconds.

1

Download & Install

Download the DMG from GitHub releases. Open it, drag Wapp into your Applications folder. Done.

2

Add Your Accounts

Click the + button, name your account, then scan the QR code with your phone. Repeat for each account.

3

Manage & Focus

Switch between accounts with tabs. Press ⌘B to hide the sidebar when you need to focus on a conversation.
